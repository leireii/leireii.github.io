Visually Aided Listening

In the year 2000, at the Millennium Summit, plans were made for all parts of the UN system to pool their combined efforts toward achieving eight common goals to make the world a better place by the year 2015. The unit's reading passage describes the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and plans for implementing them.

The unit's structural focus is on the use of separable and inseparable two-word verbs, and its writing task focuses on writing persuasively. The listening exercise continues the unit's theme, showing a UN hip-hop video explaining the Development Goals and showing the UN's commitment to fostering equality, peace, and sustainable growth.
