The Past Participle

There are individual exercises in this section.

The present perfect tense can be used to describe an action that began in the past and continues up to the present time. The present perfect is formed by conjugating the present form of the verb have with the past participle of the main verb.


Subject Correct form of have Past participle Since + specified time
The organization has existed since 1945.

In the exercise below, put the verb in parentheses in the correct form of the present perfect tense (for the purpose of this exercise, use the present perfect tense only).
