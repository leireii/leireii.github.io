Malala Day and I Will Work For Peace

facepeople
There are individual and class discussion exercises in this section.



Malala Yousafzai and Kailash Satyarthi were jointly awarded the 2014 Nobel Peace Prize "for their struggle against the suppression of children and young people and for the right of all children to education". The United Nations declared 12 July, her 16th birthday, “Malala Day” in honour of her courage and determination, and for inspiring others to speak out for their rights.

Download the exercise, read the questions, then watch the video below. After you have watched the video, work on the exercise.
