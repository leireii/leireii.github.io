One Day

face
There are individual exercises in this section.

The video you will listen to, "One Day," was made to celebrate the International Day of Peace. The International Day of Peace is celebrated each year on 21 September.

In the video, you will hear some children talking about their hopes that one day there will be a peaceful world. In the background, the former boxing champion, Muhammed Ali, says a few words.

Note: you will have to download Real Player to hear the audio.




After you have listened to the passage "One Day," see if you can review the different activities the children were doing in the video by doing the exercise.
