Grammar

assignment

-ing Verbs and Adjectives

face
There are individual exercises in this section.

Notice that some adjectives in the unit reading ended with '-ing'. Students of English sometimes think that only verbs can end in '-ing'; however, some adjectives can as well.

Take a look at the sentences below. Notice how in the first sentence, the 'ing' word is used as a verb, but in the second sentence, the 'ing' word is used as an adjective. Notice the different positions of the verb and the adjective in the sentences below:

1. The numbers of women benefiting from micro-credit programmes is increasing.
2. Increasing numbers of women are benefiting from micro-credit programmes.

Observe how the second sentence presents information more concisely.

face

Exercise


Prices are rising and putting an economic strain on many families.
prices are putting an economic strain on many families.

Demand for technical skills is growing and making specialized education a necessity
demand for technical skills is making specialized education a necessity.

The number of female heads of household is increasing and making it necessary for many women to develop marketable skills.
numbers of female heads of households are making it necessary for many women to develop marketable skills.

Some institutions are participating in micro-credit programmes and offering small loans to poor women.
institutions are offering small, "micro-credit" loans to poor women.

Some countries are developing and seeking ways to educate their populations.
Some countries are seeking ways to educate their populations.
