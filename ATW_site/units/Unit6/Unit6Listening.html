Radio Broadcasts

Listen to the two radio broadcasts below.

Broadcast 1

International Year of Languages 2008



Transcript

This is a UN radio programme about “The Speaking Tables,” an initiative of the Language and Communications Programme to celebrate the UN International Year of Languages. New York Headquarters staff comment on the first of the “Speaking Tables” events — which encourage staff to practice a second UN language during their lunch hour. Javier Zanon, dierector of this initiative, is interviewed.


Broadcast 2

UN at 60: Supporting Literacy in Uganda



Transcript

This is a radio broadcast about a visit to a literacy project in a Ugandan village. This broadcast was part of a programme titled "UN at 60: staff members share their memorable experiences with the Organization: Supporting Literacy in Uganda." The UN's Pat Duffy is interviewed.
