Action through Words

Action through Words helps users expand their knowledge of the
English language and the history of the United Nations.

What to Expect From Each Unit

info

Introduction

The Unit Introduction gives a general idea of the subject matter and exercises covered in each unit.

Reading

Reading passages allow you to improve your English reading skills and learn important information about the United Nations.

Grammar

Grammar sections in each unit present grammar points from the reading and include grammar practice activities to apply your new skills.

Writing

Writing sections for each unit provide you with example texts and helpful writing structures.

Listening sections provide challenging listening passages on topics related to the unit. In the listening sections you can also find transcripts and comprehension questions.
