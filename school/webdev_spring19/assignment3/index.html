Michi

Michi is my cat. We've had him since 2012, when a colleague of my mother's sent out an e-mail saying that he found an abandoned, almost newborn kitten near his backyard and that he couldn't keep it due to his wife not being able to be near cats. He has a very unique personality and is honestly a jerk sometimes. However, he's my son and I love him with all my heart.
