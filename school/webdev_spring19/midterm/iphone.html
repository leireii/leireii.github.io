Display Settings

Shown on Screen:
To change the text size, select Text Size and you will open a screen that allows you to change it manually by dragging your finger along the bottom selector—right for larger, left for smaller.
To reach display settings, tap the Settings icon , and scroll down to the Display & Brightness menu. Tap on the menu to open it and several options will appear.

To change the brightness of the screen, drag the symbol in the top blue-and-gray bar right to make it brighter and left to make it darker.

To toggle True Tone (which changes the colors of the screen), Raise to Wake (which activates the phone when you raise it), or Bold Text (which makes text thicker), simply tap on the switch icons . Green means activated, while white means unactivated.

To turn on Display Zoom, tap on the View menu under the Display Zoom section and you will be taken to another screen where you can toggle Display Zoom and see examples. Tap 'Done' on the top right corner when finished!

Volume Settings

Silent Mode:

To toggle Silent Mode, which makes the phone vibrate instead of ringing, flip the switch on the top left of the iPhone (shown by the arrow). If the switch is pushed towards you and the back is the color of your phone, Silent Mode is off. If it is flipped away from you and the back of the switch is red, Silent Mode is on.

Volume Up and Down:

To Change the Volume, press on either of the volume buttons that are shown by the bracket. Pressing the top button raises the volume while pressing the lower button will lower the volume. This works when Silent Mode is on or off.
To change the volume digitally, put your finger on the bottom of your screen and drag up to reveal the Control Center. Here, you will see the icon which can be tapped and dragged up or down to adjust volume.

Internet Settings
To choose a wireless Internet network, tap the Settings icon and enter the Wi-Fi menu. From here, you can find your network in the list, tap on it, and enter a password if needed.

If you want to turn on Airplane Mode, which turns off your Wi-Fi, phone service, and BlueTooth completely, you can either toggle it in Settings, or swipe up from the bottom of the screen and tap on the airplane symbol in the Control Center.

Calling

If someone is calling you, the screen will show the caller name and the option to answer. Sometimes, there will be a green and red button—in this case, press red to decline and green to answer. Other times, there might be the option to slide your finger across the screen to answer. To end a call, press the red button.

To call someone, go to the Phone app and find their name either in the Contact List or the Recents. Click on their name to call, and there might be an option to choose their phone number or FaceTime. FaceTime is a videocall option that is only available with other iPhones.
